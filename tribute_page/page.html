Kawhi Leonard

A fun guy

Here are some highlights from Kawhi's career

  • 1991 - Kawhi was born in Los Angeles, California
  • 2009-2011 - Kawhi attends SDSU on a basketball scholarship. During his time there he made first team MWC(Mountain West Conference) and led the Aztecs to two NCAA tounrament bids.
  • 2011 - Kawhi starts his rookie year with the San Antonio Spurs. Leonard was named to the All-Rookie first team
  • 2012 - Kawhi helps his team to an NBA finals appearance, but lose to the Miami Heat in 7 games
  • 2013 - Kawhi leads his team victorious in the NBA finals. Leonard averaged 17.8 points on 61% shooting and was named Finals MVP. He was the third-youngest winner of the award (22 years and 351 days), behind Magic Johnson - who broke the record twice.
  • 2014 - Kawhi emerges as the best defender in the league and wins Defensive Player of The Year
  • 2018 - Kawhi gets traded to the Toronto Raptors, and leads the team to an NBA Championship.
